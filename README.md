# Vehicle Sales, Recalls & Sentiment — IBM Cognos Analytics

An IBM Cognos Analytics dashboard analysing vehicle sales performance, recall 
incidents, and customer sentiment across dealer networks and models — with a 
deliberate risk lens built into the design.

## Why a Risk Lens?
Most sales dashboards optimise for revenue. This one surfaces recall density 
alongside commercial KPIs — because product quality failures are a leading indicator of long-term revenue risk. That framing guided every design decision.

## What I Built
- **Data module** with relationships across sales, recalls, dealers, and sentiment
- **KPI design**: profit by dealer, units sold by model, recall rate by system
- **Recall heatmap** (system × model) — a governance-oriented view of product quality risk
- **Customer sentiment treemap**: positive / neutral / negative distribution
- **Mobile-optimised layout** for field and executive use


## My Role in Analytics 
- **Data Preparation:** Cleaned and structured incoming data; defined initial transformation rules.
- **Data Modelling:** Built the data module with relationships between sales, recalls, dealers, and sentiment.
- **KPI & Metrics Calculation:** Created measures for profit, quantity sold, recalls per model/system, and sentiment breakdown.
- **Dashboard Design:** Built and iterated on visualisations, layout, and a mobile version.
- **UX Considerations:** Selected appropriate charts and interactivity for clarity and usability.
