# Vehicle Sales & Recalls Dashboard – IBM Cognos

This repository contains my **first IBM Cognos Analytics dashboards**, built as part of the IBM Data Visualization course.  
They demonstrate my early experience in **data analysis, data modelling, KPI definition and dashboard design** using Cognos.

## Objective
Analyse vehicle sales, recalls, and customer sentiment to uncover patterns and inform decision-making.  


## Dataset
Sample dataset provided during the IBM Data Visualization course containing:
- Vehicle sales by model and dealer
- Recall incidents by month, system, and model
- Customer sentiment data

All screenshots and data shown here are from anonymised or sample data provided for learning purposes.

## My Role as Data Analyst
- **Data Preparation:** Cleaned and structured incoming data; defined initial transformation rules.
- **Data Modelling:** Built the data module with relationships between sales, recalls, dealers, and sentiment.
- **KPI & Metrics Calculation:** Created measures for profit, quantity sold, recalls per model/system, and sentiment breakdown.
- **Dashboard Design:** Built and iterated on visualisations, layout, and a mobile version.
- **UX Considerations:** Selected appropriate charts and interactivity for clarity and usability.

## Dashboard Features
### Dashboard 1
- **# of Recalls by Model**: Bar chart of total recalls per vehicle model.
- **Customer Sentiment**: Treemap showing positive, neutral, and negative sentiment distribution.
- **Quantity of Cars Sold by Month vs Profit**: Combined chart tracking sales and profit over time.
- **Recalls by Affected System and Model**: Heatmap showing recall density across systems and models.

### Dashboard 2
- **Profit & Quantity Sold KPIs**: Large KPI cards showing total profit and quantity sold.
- **Quantity Sold by Model**: Horizontal bar chart comparing units sold per model.
- **Profit by Dealer ID**: Bar chart showing profit distribution by dealer.
